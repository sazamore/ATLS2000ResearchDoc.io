📋 Assignment Instructions
Click to expand
▾
  • Identify a topic that genuinely interests and motivates you.
  • Form a good research question and include it in the Research Focus Table.
  • Research topics that help answer your question using reliable sources.
  • Complete the Sources Table — at least 3 sources with 3 facts each.
  • Choose one (1) track in the Research Focus Table and complete all rows.
  • Complete the Artist Statement if your deliverable is standalone or intangible (poem, song, sculpture, etc.).
  • Complete the Required AI Reporting section.
  • Create a deliverable you enjoy making and feel proud of.

Your research will support a Creative Deliverable that addresses your research question in an arguable way — one that spurs conversation, not just informs. It should not be a regurgitation of sources, but what you take away from them. The goal is to engage, inspire, and help others make new meaning from the topic.
